Introducing Om Auto and Tyres: Your One-Stop Shop for Two-Wheeler Needs and More!

Welcome to Om Auto and Tyres, your trusted destination for all things related to two-wheelers and beyond! Since our establishment in 1995, we have been serving the automotive community with passion and dedication, providing top-notch products and services that keep vehicles running smoothly.

At Om Auto and Tyres, we take pride in offering an extensive range of high-quality two-wheeler tyres that ensure superior grip, enhanced performance, and increased safety on the road. Whether you are a biking enthusiast or a daily commuter, our diverse selection of tyres will cater to your specific needs and preferences.

In addition to tyres, we understand the importance of proper maintenance for optimal vehicle performance. That's why we also stock a comprehensive array of oils and grease, carefully selected to meet the requirements of various vehicles. Our knowledgeable team is always ready to assist you in choosing the right lubricants, helping you keep your two-wheeler or other vehicles in prime condition.

But that's not all! As your ultimate automotive partner, we offer a wide range of two-wheeler spare parts, ensuring that you find the perfect components to replace and upgrade as needed. Our collection includes genuine and reliable spare parts that guarantee the longevity and efficiency of your beloved ride.

Om Auto and Tyres doesn't stop at serving just two-wheelers; we also cater to the needs of tractor owners with our specialized tractor trolley spare parts. Whether you require components for repairs or improvements, we've got you covered with a selection that meets industry standards and surpasses expectations.

What sets us apart is our commitment to providing exceptional customer service. Our team comprises automotive enthusiasts with in-depth knowledge and expertise, ready to assist you in finding the right products and solutions for your vehicles.

Visit Om Auto and Tyres today and experience a world of automotive excellence under one roof. Whether you are a seasoned rider, a tractor owner, or simply seeking top-quality vehicle products, we guarantee a satisfying shopping experience. Trust in us to keep your wheels rolling with reliability and precision.
